Heathers

The movie Heathers, which came out in 1989, featured actress Winona Ryder. The movie is rather messed, with the ethics of the main character, Veronica, being fudged a bit by her new boyfriend JD, who not sees no problem in killing someone and faking it as a suicide, but having the entire school sign a petition that (unbeknownst to them) actually discusses a suicide pact--a suicide pact that JD plans on making come true by blowing up the school during a pep rally. Overall, the movie is very good although it is also very messed up. Suicide and murder was a theme throughout the entirety of the movie.
