Edward Scissorhands

Edward Scissorhands, featuring Johnny Depp and Winona Ryder, came out in 1990. In the movie, Edward Scissorhands is built by a scientist who dies before finishing building Edward. Edward is then taken home by some woman named Peg, and he then falls in love with her teenage daughter (honestly the age gap is sort of creepy, I looked it up and Johnny Depp was 27 or so, and Winona Ryder was only 18, this is a common theme in movies, men being far older than their partner.
